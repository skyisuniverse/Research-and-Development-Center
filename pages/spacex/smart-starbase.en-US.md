# Smart Starbase

Imagine if the concept of Smart Starbase was possible?

Currently there are a lot of different factors that are in some way influencing Starship flights and their execution. Think: 

- Starship components / parts and their reliability, 
- (evolving) composition / structure / design of starships
- necessity to handle propellant ops and maintain prop (maintaining, propellant settling, attitude control (for 12 hours))

There could be hundreds to thousands of changes (including software and hardware, (human factor?))

With a Smart Starbase, all Starship OPs could be made preditable, reliable and execution smooth.

---

What if a Smart Starbase could handle:

- Starship launch
- Booster Catch
- Ship Catch

automatically?
