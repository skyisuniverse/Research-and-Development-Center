# Nano-photonics

Nanophotonics is the science of manipulating light at the nanometer scale, merging nanotechnology with photonics to control light-matter interactions below the diffraction limit. It enables the miniaturization of optical components, allowing for smaller, faster, and more efficient devices for computing, data storage, and sensing.
