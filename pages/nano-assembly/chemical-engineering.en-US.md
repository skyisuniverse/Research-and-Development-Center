# Chemical Engineering

> Chemical engineering is a versatile discipline that applies physical sciences (chemistry, physics) and biology with mathematics to design processes that convert raw materials into valuable, everyday products.

## Key Aspects of Chemical Engineering

- Process Engineering: Designing, simulating, and optimizing manufacturing processes for commercial production.

- Core Principles: Involves thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, heat/mass transfer, and reaction kinetics.

- Key Skills: Using simulation tools (e.g., CAD, ASPEN) and applying principles to turn lab-scale inventions into industrial-scale production.
