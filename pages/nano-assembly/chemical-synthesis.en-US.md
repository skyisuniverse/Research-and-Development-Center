> **Chemical synthesis** (chemical combination) is the artificial execution of **chemical reactions** to obtain one or more **products**. This occurs by **physical** and **chemical** **manipulations**, usually involving one or more **reactions**. (@) Wikipedia

> **Chemical synthesis** is the production of **chemical compounds** from simpler materials through controlled **chemical reactions**, enabling the **construction of** complex, defined **molecules** with targeted **properties**. (@) Grokipedia
